## What counts as commercial use?

Any use that is predominantly or primarily directed toward commercial advantage,
monetary compensation, or other commercial benefit. Examples:

- Installing Voboost on vehicles that are sold, leased, or rented.
- Bundling Voboost into a commercial product, firmware, or distribution.
- Offering Voboost installation as a paid service (e.g., dealerships, repair shops).
- Using Voboost internally in a commercial fleet or commercial operations.

## What is allowed under the noncommercial license?

- Personal installation on your own vehicle.
- Studying, modifying, and sharing the source code.
- Contributing improvements back to the project.

allows us to continue building and improving the project while keeping it
free for individual enthusiasts.
